- in format CATEGORY{ word:freq}* using package parsers + SentenceProcessing
- test-train split using DataSplitter
- creating a vocabulary using Vocabulary
- filter that vocabulary (no stopwords, no words with frequency of 2 or less, 
no words with digits in them) with VocabularyFilter (reduction by half), this also
removes these unnecessary words out of the training and testing files
- if your training and testing instances are not sorted, this is the moment to do so, 
using ExplicitDataSorter
- transform each word frequency pair to an index frequency pair using the WordIndexer
